About

01 / 05

A system as a whole — software to silicon.

I study Electrical & Electronics Engineering at NIT Calicut, building a skill set that spans core engineering fundamentals, programming and hardware implementation. My work covers Circuit Simulation, Digital Circuit Design, Embedded System Programming and ML- Model Development.

The most interesting problems live at the boundary, where a carefully-designed hardware subsystem hands off to an intelligent software layer. Whether that's an elevator controller arbitrating floor priority through pure logic ICs or a convolutional network classifying galaxy morphologies, I try to own the whole stack. An Opportunity to Work at the Intersection of Hardware and Software is because of my degree and my interest aligning in this field.
